Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize the PDF layouts? expand_more
Yes, Basic and Premium plans allow full customization of your proforma templates, including logo upload, brand colors, and specific data field visibility.
Is my financial data secure? expand_more
Absolutely. We utilize bank-level AES-256 encryption for all data at rest and in transit. Our infrastructure is audited regularly for compliance with enterprise security standards.
How does the WhatsApp sharing work? expand_more
We integrate directly with the WhatsApp API. With a single click from the dashboard, a secure link to the PDF is generated and sent to your client's registered WhatsApp number along with a customizable message.
